Key Benefits of Location Safety

Our GPS feature is more than just a dot on a map. It's a comprehensive tool designed to give you actionable insights and enhanced security.

Real-Time GPS

See your child's exact location updated in near real-time. The high-accuracy GPS data ensures you have the most current information available.

Location History

Review the paths your child has taken. See a detailed history of their movements throughout the day, complete with timestamps.

Safe & Unsafe Zones (Geofencing)

Create virtual boundaries around important places like home and school. Receive instant alerts when your child enters or leaves these designated zones.
