Never Miss an Important Alert

A child's phone is constantly buzzing with notifications that tell a story about their social life, interests, and potential risks. With FamToolApp's Notification Review, you can see the same alerts your child sees, from every app on their phone. This includes new messages on WhatsApp, friend requests on Instagram, updates from games, and much more. It's a simple, powerful way to stay connected and aware of their digital world.
