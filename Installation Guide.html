Installation Guide

Follow these simple, visual steps to set up NexusControl on your child's Android device and start monitoring.

STEP 1

Create Your Parent Account

First, create a secure account. You can sign up directly from our Parent Panel using any web browser. You can also register from the app on your child's phone after installation.

Parent Panel

STEP 2

Disable Google Play Protect

Before installing the app, it's crucial to disable Google Play Protect on the child's device. This prevents Android from blocking the app's monitoring features. Open the Play Store, tap your profile, then go to Play Protect and tap the settings gear to turn it off.

STEP 3

Install, Login & Allow Permissions

Now, install the APK. After opening the app, sign in with your parent account. The app will then ask for essential permissions like Location, SMS, and Camera access. Please allow these for the app to function correctly.

Welcome Back

STEP 4

Enable Required Services

In the final step, you need to enable the necessary system services for the app to function. Tapping on each option will take you to the phone's settings. There, you'll need to find **NexusControl** and turn the service 'On'. Once all services are enabled, click the 'Hide App' button to make the application invisible to your child.
