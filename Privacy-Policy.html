Our Privacy Commitment

At FamToolApp ("we", "our"), the privacy of you and your family is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

FamToolApp is designed to provide parents with a parental guidance tool for their children's safety. By the nature of the service, the application collects the following types of information from the child's device and transmits it to the parent's secure panel:

  • Personal Identifiers: Device name, user name.
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS coordinates and location history.
  • Communication Logs: SMS messages (incoming and outgoing), call logs.
  • Multimedia Files: Photos and videos stored on the device.
  • App Activity: Keystroke data (typed characters) and notifications from various apps, including social media.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and other technical details.

2. Data Security and Encryption

We take the security of your data extremely seriously. Our security measures are ironclad:

Impenetrable End-to-End Encryption

All data traveling from the child's device to our servers and then to your parent panel is secured using state-of-the-art end-to-end encryption. This data exists in an encrypted state that cannot be intercepted, read, or recorded by anyone in between.

Unique Decryption Key

This encrypted data is only decrypted when you, the parent, log into your account with your correct email and password. Your login credential is the sole key that transforms this data into a viewable format. No one else, including FamToolApp employees, can view this data.

No Third-Party Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share any of your personal or collected information with any third parties. Your data is for your use only. Our business model is not based on selling your privacy.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with our parental guidance services, allowing you to review your child's activities.
  • To improve, maintain, and secure our services.
  • To send you important updates, security alerts, and support messages.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We enforce strict data retention policies to uphold your privacy:

  • Automatic Deletion: To maximize security and privacy, all collected data stored on our servers is automatically and permanently deleted every seven (7) days.
  • Real-Time Account Deletion: If you choose to delete or close your child's account from your dashboard, our algorithm immediately removes all encrypted data associated with that account from our servers in real-time. No residual copies are kept.

5. Account Management and Inactivity

To ensure the integrity and security of our service, the following conditions apply:

  • Permission Failure: If not all required permissions are granted or settings are incorrectly configured on the child's device for the application to function properly, our system may be unable to provide the service. In such cases, our algorithm may temporarily suspend or permanently close the account for security reasons.
  • Inactivity Policy: If a parent does not log into their account for an extended period (typically over 30 days), the account may be deemed inactive by our algorithm. As a security measure, the inactive account and all its associated data may be permanently deleted.

6. Legal Requirements and Responsible Use

FamToolApp is intended for legal and ethical purposes only, such as parental guidance of your own children for whom you are the legal guardian. It is the user's responsibility to comply with all applicable laws in their country regarding the use of such software. You must inform the owner of the device that their activities are being reviewed for safety purposes.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
