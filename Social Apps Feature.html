Social Apps Safety

Help your child navigate conversations and activities across popular social media platforms safely.

Feature Status: Beta Testing

Coming Soon to All Users!

The ability to review social media apps like WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook is one of our most requested features, and we're excited to announce it's on its way! This powerful tool will allow you to read chat messages, view shared media, and understand your child's social interactions to protect them from online risks.

An Exclusive First Look

This feature is currently in the final stages of testing and is in beta version. We have given access to a select group of users to help us refine and perfect its functionality.

Once the testing is complete, the Social Apps Safety feature will be automatically available in your parent panel. We are working hard to make it available to everyone very soon. Thank you for your patience!
