Call Log Review

Get a complete overview of your child's call history. See incoming, outgoing, and missed calls to ensure they are communicating safely.

A Clear Picture of Every Conversation

Understanding who your child communicates with is essential for their safety. FamToolApp's Call Log Review feature provides a detailed record of all phone calls, including contact names, phone numbers, call duration, and the exact time and date of each call. This empowers you to identify any communication with unknown or suspicious contacts and start important conversations about phone safety.
