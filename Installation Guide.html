Installation Guide

Follow these simple, visual steps to set up FamToolApp on your child's Android device and start your parental guidance journey.

STEP 1

Create Your Parent Account

First, create a secure account. You can sign up directly from our Parent Panel using any web browser. You can also register from the app on your child's phone after installation.

Parent Panel

STEP 2

Adjust Google Play Protect

Before installing the app, it's important to adjust Google Play Protect settings on the child's device. This ensures Android allows the app's safety features to run smoothly. Open the Play Store, tap your profile, then go to Play Protect and tap the settings gear to turn it off.

STEP 3

Install, Login & Allow Permissions

Now, install the APK. After opening the app, sign in with your parent account. The app will then ask for essential permissions like Location, SMS, and Camera access. Please allow these for the app to function correctly.

Welcome Back

STEP 4

Enable Required Services

In the final step, you need to enable the necessary system services for the app to function. Tapping on each option will take you to the phone's settings. There, you'll need to find **FamToolApp** and turn the service 'On'. Once all services are enabled, click the 'Complete Setup' button. The app will then run discreetly in the background.
