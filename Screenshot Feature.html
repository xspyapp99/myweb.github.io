Remote Screenshot

Instantly capture what's on your child's screen, no matter what app they are using.

Feature Status: Beta Testing

Coming Soon to All Users!

See exactly what your child sees. Our upcoming Screenshot feature will allow you to remotely capture a high-quality image of the phone's current screen with a single click from your dashboard. This is perfect for understanding the context of a conversation, viewing a post on social media, or checking their activity in any app.

An Exclusive First Look

This feature is currently in the final stages of testing and is in **beta version**. We have given access to a select group of users to help us refine and perfect its functionality.

Once the testing is complete, the Screenshot feature will be automatically available in your parent panel. We are working hard to make it available to everyone very soon. Thank you for your patience!
