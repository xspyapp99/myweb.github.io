Please follow the instructions below carefully to download and install the app.

A Note on Play Protect

This is an important step for a smooth installation. Our app includes advanced safety features that require specific permissions to work correctly. Google's security system (Play Protect) may sometimes flag powerful apps like ours.

Our app is 100% safe for its intended purpose. To ensure a successful installation, we recommend temporarily adjusting Play Protect settings. You can do this easily by following the steps below.

How to Adjust Play Protect:

  1. Open the Play Store app on the child's phone.
  2. Tap the profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select Play Protect from the menu.
  4. Tap the Settings (⚙️) icon and turn off "Scan apps with Play Protect".

Responsible Usage and Legal Agreement
